Pa. Vijay (பா. விஜய்)

Vijay, whose full name is Pamarthi Vijayakumar, is a prominent figure in the Tamil film industry, renowned for his multifaceted talents as a poet, lyricist, and actor. He has collaborated with many leading composers and singers in the industry.In addition to his work as a lyricist, Pa. Vijay has ventured into acting and filmmaking.

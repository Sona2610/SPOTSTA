Name :A. R. Rahman(ஏ. ஆர். ரகுமான்)

A.R. Rahman, born as A. S. Dileep Kumar on January 6, 1967, in Chennai, India, is a globally acclaimed composer, singer, and music producer. Rahman's journey in the world of music is nothing short of legendary. He burst onto the Indian music scene in the early 1990s and swiftly rose to prominence with his groundbreaking work. Rahman's music is characterized by its eclectic fusion of Indian classical music, electronic sounds, and Western orchestration, creating a unique and captivating sonic tapestry. His ability to seamlessly blend diverse musical elements has earned him the moniker "Mozart of Madras."Rahman gained international recognition with his work on the soundtrack of the film "Roja" (1992), directed by Mani Ratnam. The soundtrack's success marked the beginning of Rahman's illustrious career and set a new standard for film music in India. Since then, Rahman has composed music for numerous Indian films across various languages, winning numerous awards and accolades along the way, including multiple National Film Awards, Filmfare Awards, and a record-breaking two Academy Awards

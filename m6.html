Harris Jeyaraj(இளையராஜா)

Name : Harris Jeyaraj

Harris Jayaraj, born on January 8, 1975, in Chennai, India, is a highly acclaimed music composer known for his contributions to Indian cinema, particularly in the Tamil and Telugu film industries. Harris Jayaraj began his musical journey at a young age, learning the intricacies of music composition and production. He gained initial recognition as a keyboardist and programmer for renowned composers before making his debut as an independent music director.His ability to craft music that complements the mood and narrative of films has earned him widespread acclaim and numerous awards.Since his debut, Harris Jayaraj has delivered hit soundtracks for numerous Tamil and Telugu films, collaborating with leading filmmakers and actors in the industry. Some of his notable works include "Minnale" (2001), "Kaakha Kaakha" (2003), "Ghajini" (2005), "Vaaranam Aayiram" (2008), and "Anegan" (2015), among others. His songs often become chart-toppers and remain popular among music lovers for years to come.

Name : Vijay Antony(விஜய் ஆண்டனி)

Vijay Anthony, born on July 24, 1975, in Tamil Nadu, India, is a multi-talented personality in the Indian film industry, known for his contributions as a music composer, actor, and playback singer. Initially trained in classical music, Vijay Anthony ventured into the world of music composition and made his debut as a music director in the Tamil film industry.Vijay Anthony gained widespread recognition for his work as a music composer with his compositions often characterized by their melodious tunes and emotional depth. He composed music for several successful Tamil films, earning praise for his ability to capture the essence of the narratives through his music. In addition to his work as a composer, Vijay Anthony transitioned into acting, making his debut as a lead actor in the Tamil film "Naan" (2012). He received critical acclaim for his performance and subsequently appeared in several other films in lead roles. His performances often portray complex characters with shades of grey, showcasing his versatility as an actor

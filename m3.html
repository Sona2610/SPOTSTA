Name : Yuvan Shankar Raja (யுவன் சங்கர் ராஜா)

Yuvan Shankar Raja, born on August 31, 1979, in Chennai, India, is a prominent music composer and singer in the Indian film industry, particularly in Tamil cinema. He is the third and youngest child of the legendary Indian music composer, Ilaiyaraaja. Yuvan Shankar Raja inherited his father's musical genes and began his career in music at a young age, making his debut as a composer in the Tamil film "Aravindhan" (1997).Yuvan's music is known for its versatility and contemporary appeal. He has a unique style that blends elements of various genres, including rock, hip-hop, electronic, and folk, creating a distinct sound that resonates with audiences across generations. His ability to experiment with different musical styles while maintaining a strong emotional connect has earned him a dedicated fan base.Yuvan Shankar Raja rose to prominence in the early 2000s with his hit soundtracks for films like "7G Rainbow Colony" (2004), "Pudhupettai" (2006), and "Billa" (2007). His compositions, characterized by catchy tunes and evocative lyrics, have become synonymous with the modern Tamil film music scene.

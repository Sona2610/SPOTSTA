Anirudh Ravichander (அனிருத் ரவிச்சந்திரன்)

Name : Anirudh Ravichander (அனிருத் ரவிச்சந்திரன்)

Anirudh Ravichander, born on October 16, 1990, in Chennai, India, is a prolific music composer, singer, and songwriter primarily working in the Tamil and Telugu film industries. Anirudh shot to fame with his debut soundtrack for the Tamil film "3" (2012), directed by Aishwarya R. Dhanush. The song "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the film became a viral sensation and catapulted Anirudh to stardom, making him an overnight sensation.Anirudh's music is characterized by its contemporary sound, catchy melodies, and youthful vibe. He has a knack for blending various musical genres, including pop, rock, electronic, and hip-hop, to create chart-topping hits that resonate with audiences of all ages. His innovative approach to music production and composition has earned him a dedicated fan following.Since his debut, Anirudh has consistently delivered hit soundtracks for a wide range of Tamil and Telugu films. Some of his notable works include "Ethir Neechal" (2013), "Maari" (2015), "Vedalam" (2015), "Remo" (2016), and "Master" (2021), among others.

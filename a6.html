SPOT STAR - Kamal Hassan Movies

Kamal Haasan, often referred to as the "Ulaga Nayagan" (Universal Hero) and "Ulaganayagan" (World Hero), is one of the most versatile and celebrated actors in Indian cinema. His career, spanning over five decades, encompasses a wide range of roles and genres, earning him numerous awards and accolades.Born into a family with a strong background in the performing arts, Kamal Haasan began his acting career as a child artist and quickly rose to prominence with his remarkable talent and dedication. He made his debut as a lead actor in the 1970s and went on to establish himself as one of the finest actors of his generation. Kamal Haasan's versatility as an actor is unparalleled. He has effortlessly portrayed a diverse array of characters, from intense dramas to rib-tickling comedies, and from action-packed thrillers to thought-provoking social dramas. His ability to immerse himself completely into his roles and bring depth and authenticity to his characters has earned him critical acclaim and adoration from audiences worldwide.

Vaali (வாலி)

Occupation : Artist,poet,lyricist,writer,actor.Vaali, whose full name was T. S. Rangarajan, was a legendary lyricist in the Tamil film industry, revered for his prolific career spanning several decades. Born on October 29, 1931, Vaali's contributions to Tamil cinema have left an indelible mark on the industry.aali was born in Srirangam, Tamil Nadu, India. His passion for poetry and literature blossomed at a young age, leading him to pursue a career in writing. He started his career as a poet, contributing verses to various magazines and publications. His talent for writing soon caught the attention of filmmakers in the Tamil industry.Vaali made his mark as a lyricist in the Tamil film industry, penning thousands of songs that became iconic in Tamil cinema. His lyrical compositions were characterized by their depth, emotion, and poetic beauty. He collaborated with numerous music directors and composers, including M. S. Viswanathan, Ilaiyaraaja, and A. R. Rahman, among others, creating timeless melodies that resonated with audiences.

Sivakarthikeyan (born 17 February 1985), also known by the initialism SK, is an Indian actor, singer, film producer, lyricist, and television presenter, who works predominantly in Tamil films. He is a recipient of a Tamil Nadu State Film Award, four Edison Awards, four SIIMA Awards, and two other Vijay Award.In the colorful tapestry of Indian cinema, Sivakarthikeyan shines as a multi-talented gem. With his infectious energy, charismatic presence, and remarkable versatility, he has carved a niche for himself in the hearts of millions. Let's delve into the journey of this dynamic actor, comedian, playback singer, and television presenter who has captivated audiences with his innate charm and talent.Sivakarthikeyan was born in Singampuneri, a small town in Tamil Nadu, India. He completed his education in engineering and worked as a graphic designer before venturing into the entertainment industry. Sivakarthikeyan's journey in the limelight began with his participation in various reality TV shows as a contestant and anchor, notably in the popular show "Kalakka Povathu Yaaru."
